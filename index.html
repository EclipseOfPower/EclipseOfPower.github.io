Eclipse of Power: The Dark Ascendancy

Author: Naird12

Synopsis

In a galaxy ruled by order, chaos is a force both feared and forbidden. Yet, for Nero, chaos is the key to ultimate power.
Scarred by a tragic past and driven by an insatiable hunger for strength, he will stop at nothing to seize it.

He steals, destroys, and kills without hesitation, carving a path of devastation in his relentless pursuit of immortality.

With the ancient Polykenas at his command—beings born from chaos itself—Nero has discovered a way to harness the untapped energy of fallen worlds.

His empire grows, and his enemies fall before him.
But as he edges closer to godhood, the question remains: Will he control the chaos, or will it consume him?
In this dark tale of power and ambition, even the gods will tremble before the strongest of immortals.
